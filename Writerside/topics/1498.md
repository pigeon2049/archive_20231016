# GraphQL Conf 2023

**2023-10-16 04:47**

**https://bartwullems.blogspot.com/2023/10/graphql-conf-2023.html?ref=dailydev**

GraphQLConf 2023 has concluded, but you can watch session recordings and access speaker slides. Highlights include Uri Goldshtein's keynote on the state of GraphQL and Michael Staib's presentation on GraphQL Fusion, an open spec for remote execution and federation of services. The event also covers the combination of GraphQL and CQRS, as well as the presence of AI in the GraphQL ecosystem.
